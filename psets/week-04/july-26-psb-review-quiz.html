Questions 1-6 - Weekly ISI-Style Review
1. Method of indicators review: isolated singletons
ReviewMethod of indicators

\(n\) balls are independently placed into \(n\) labelled boxes arranged in a line. Let \(X\) count boxes that contain exactly one ball and have no occupied immediate neighbour. Set up \(X\) using indicators and derive \(E[X]\), separating endpoints from interior boxes.

2. Conditional expectation review: random sample maximum
ReviewConditional expectation

Given \(N\sim\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda)\), take \(N\) iid Uniform\((0,1)\) observations and define \(M=0\) if \(N=0\). Compute \(P(M\le x)\) for \(0\le x\le1\), naming the conditioning variable.

4. MLE review: support-dependent estimator bias
ReviewMLE

\(X_1,\ldots,X_n\) are iid Uniform\((0,\theta)\). Write the likelihood, find the MLE, and give an unbiased correction based on the MLE.

5. UMP review: exponential rate tail direction
ReviewUMP/NP

One observation has exponential density \(\lambda e^{-\lambda x}\). For \(H_0:\lambda=\lambda_0\) against \(H_1:\lambda=\lambda_1>\lambda_0\), write the likelihood ratio and identify the most powerful rejection-region shape.

6. Regression review: residual projection identity
ReviewRegression and OLS

In matrix OLS with full-column-rank \(X\), define \(H=X(X^TX)^{-1}X^T\) and \(e=(I-H)y\). Prove that \(e\) is orthogonal to the fitted vector \(Hy\), then state the squared-length decomposition.
